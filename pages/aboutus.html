About Us

White Label Holding LLC launched its operations in 2015 as a Buy Here Pay Here (BHPH) dealership in Miami, Florida. Since its inception, the company has steadily expanded its presence in the auto finance sector, evolving into a diversified and dynamic holding group.

Over the years, it has integrated a range of complementary business units, including a reinsurance company, a warranty division, and a portfolio management service, among others.

Today, White Label oversees an extensive network comprising both owned and independent dealerships, supported by its proprietary vehicle warehouse and logistics infrastructure. With a dedicated team of over 30 professionals across all divisions, the company manages a portfolio of more than 1,000 active accounts.

Driven by a specialized and highly experienced team within each business unit, White Label has forged strategic partnerships with industry-leading companies to continually elevate its service offerings.

At its core, the company remains committed to leveraging technological innovation and advancing financial education as cornerstones of its long-term growth strategy and impactful contributions.

White Label Holding LLC continues to scale its operations with a vision of empowering industries and communities, transforming the landscape of auto finance, and delivering sustainable solutions built for the future.
